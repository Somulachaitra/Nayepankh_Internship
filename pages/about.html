About NayePankh Foundation

One of India's biggest student-led NGOs, working with love and AI-powered intelligence to uplift underprivileged communities.

Our Mission

Empowering lives, transforming futures.

NayePankh Foundation is a non-governmental organization with a strong desire to help society and make it a better place for all. Founded in 2020, we've grown into one of India's largest student-led organizations.

Our operations extend across Kanpur, Ghaziabad, Lucknow, Delhi, and various other cities. With the integration of AI-powered tools, we now reach communities faster, smarter, and with greater impact than ever before.

  • ✓

Our Journey

From a small idea to a movement

Six years of impact, growth, and transformation.

2020

The Beginning

Founded by Prashant Shukla in Kanpur with a small team of 5 student volunteers. First food drive served 50 families.

2021

Expansion to Ghaziabad & Lucknow

Reached 10,000+ lives. Launched our flagship "Padho Padhao" education program and "Sakhi" hygiene initiative.

2022

50,000 Lives Milestone

Crossed 50K beneficiaries. Received official UP Government NGO registration and 80G tax exemption certification.

2023

100+ Active Volunteers

Built a nationwide student volunteer network. Launched "Skill Shakti" vocational training across 5 cities.

2024

2,00,000 Lives Impacted

Major milestone! Expanded to 10+ cities. Partnered with corporate CSR programs for sustainable growth.

2026

🤖 AI-Powered Transformation

Integrated cutting-edge AI features: chatbot assistant, personalized impact tracking, smart volunteer matching, and predictive community analytics.
