Padho Padhao – Education for All

Our flagship education program offers free tuition centers, school supply distribution, and digital literacy workshops for underprivileged children across 25+ centers in 10 cities. We've supported 15,000+ students in continuing their education — many of whom are now in colleges and pursuing successful careers.
